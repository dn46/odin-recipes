Pulled Pork

It will just melt in your mouth!

This may well be the juiciest, most flavourful pulled pork you ever have in your life! Homemade BBQ Sauce totally makes it. And there's no denying that using a slow cooker yields the juiciest pulled pork! BIG BATCH recipe, 100% perfect for freezing, ideal for entertaining!

Ingredients

  • 2 medium onions, thinly slice
  • 1 (4-pound) pork shoulder roast
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 (16 ounce) bottle barbecue sauce
  • 1 cup coarsely chopped onion
  • 8 to 10 hamburger buns, for serving
  • 2 cups coleslaw, for serving

Steps

  1. Place half of the thinly sliced onions in the bottom of the slow cooker.
  2. Add pork and water, along with the remaining onion slices.
  3. Cover and cook the pork on low for 7 to 9 hours or 4 to 5 hours on the high heat setting.
  4. Drain liquid from the slow cooker.
  5. Then shred or chop the meat coarsely, and discard excess fat.
  6. Put the pork back in the slow cooker. Add barbecue sauce and chopped onion.
  7. Cover and cook on low for 2 to 4 hours longer. Stir occasionally.
  8. Serve with warm split buns and coleslaw.
