Potato Salad

A beautiful home made potato salad

This recipe is quite straightforward, although there is a fair amount of chopping involved. But once that's out of the way all you have to do is combine all the ingredients and stir thoroughly.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium salad potatoes
  • 2 medium carrots
  • 4 eggs
  • 6 small pickled gherkins
  • 5 tbsp thick yougurt
  • 5 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Place the potatoes and carrots in a pot, add water and bring to the boil. Then simmer until tender. You can boil the eggs in the same pot and remove them after 6-8 minutes. Drain and set aside to cool, then peel and chop the ingredients into small cubes and place in a large bowl. Finely chop the guerkins and add into the bowl.
  2. Add the yogurt, mayo, mustard and seasoning to the mixture and stir carefully but thoroughly. Refrigerate, covered, for up to 2 days. If possible chill for 1 hour before serving.
